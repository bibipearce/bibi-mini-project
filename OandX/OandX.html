Noughts and Crosses!

Click for instructions:
  • This is a two player, pass and play, game (played on one device)

  • Each player takes a turn to add either an X or an O to the board

  • The first player to line up three of their symbols wins

  • Symbols can be lined up vertically, horizontally or diagonally to win

  • Enter your names to get personalised messages

  • Good luck and enjoy!
